Pan-Seared Salmon

Description

This Pan-Seared Salmon recipe describes an easy step by step procedure
on how to cook Salmon on the stove. The Salmon fillets are simply seasoned with salt, pepper, and capers.
Garnish with slices of lemon.

Ingredients

  • 4(6ounce) fillets salmon
  • 2 tablespoons oliver oil
  • 2 tablespoons capers
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 slices lemon

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Preheat a large heavy skillet over medium heat for 3 minutes.

  2. Step 2

    Coat salmon fillets with olive oil; place skin-side down in the preheated skillet and increase heat to high.
    Sprinkle with capers, salt, and pepper; cook for 3 minutes on one side.
    Turn salmon fillets over; conntinue to cook until salmon flakes easily with a fork, about 5 minutes.

  3. Step 3

    Transfer salmon to individual plates and garnish with lemon slices
